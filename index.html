Shizhen Chang

Postdoctoral Researcher

üìß: szchang@ieee.org

I am a post-doctoral researcher at the Institute of Advanced Research in Artificial Intelligence (IARAI), specifically working in the AI4RS Group with Dr. Prof. Pedram Ghamisi in Austria. My background includes earning a Ph.D. degree in Photogrammetry and Remote Sensing from Wuhan University, where I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Liangpei Zhang and Prof. Bo Du.

My expertise lies in the field of deep learning and machine learning for image and signal processing. I have a strong focus on tackling a wide range of detection problems, including change detection, anomaly/outlier detection, and copy-move forgery detection. My experience extends to working with various types of data, such as optical satellite images, natural images, and high-dimensional instance data.
